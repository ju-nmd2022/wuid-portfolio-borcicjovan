Jönköping City 🇸🇪

The fun awaits

Jönköping is a small town located in the central southern part of Sweden. With population over 90 000 people, you would think the city may not have much to offer, but you would be mistaken! With Jönköping University being in the center of hte city, over 10 000 students bring life to the campus ant to the city itself by hosting parties every weekend.

What else can you do here if you are not a student and maybe don't like parties? You can always go ice-skating, and it's free! ⛸ Besides that, the views are spectacular here, so a bike stroll by the lakeside or a cup of coffe overwieving it sounds lik a perfect afternoon, doesn't it?
